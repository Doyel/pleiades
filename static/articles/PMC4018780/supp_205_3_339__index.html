    Chk2-deficient cells exit mitosis in high nocodazole. BE cells stably expressing H2B-GFP (green) were treated with 3.32 µM nocodazole in the presence of Chk2 inhibitor II and analyzed by time-lapse fluorescence microscopy using a fluorescence microscope (DMIRE2; Leica). Frames were taken every 5 min for 40 min.
